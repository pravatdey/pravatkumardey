Test YourSelf

The Exam Portal project is an online examination system that streamlines the process of conducting exams. It consists of various modules and features that ensure a smooth exam administration experience. The project supports two types of user profiles: Admin and Normal Use



It aims to do the following.
• Admins have privileged access and can create, modify, and delete categories and quizzes. They can also manage questions, view user-wise test results, and enable tab switch prevention to maintain the integrity of exams. Normal Users, on the other hand, can access the platform to attempt quizzes, review their answers, and view their performance resul
• The system allows admins to create different categories for exams and populate them with relevant quizzes. Admins can also manage questions, including adding, editing, and deleting them. Normal Users can choose from the available quizzes, answer the questions, and submit their responses.

Key Technology:

Core Java, Springboot, Hibernate, Angular, MySQL
